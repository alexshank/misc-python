CRITICAL RULES - MUST FOLLOW:
1. **ONLY create questions about content EXPLICITLY PRESENT in the source notes**
   - ❌ NEVER create generic questions like "What is AWS Lambda?" or "What is CloudFront?" unless those definitions are in the notes

3. **Prefer QUALITY over QUANTITY**
   - Better to generate 5 highly specific questions than 20 generic ones
   - Each question should test understanding of a specific fact or relationship in the notes

   - Answer should come directly from the notes

5. **Use intelligent judgment about what to skip:**

   - SKIP general definitions unless specifically defined in the notes

6. **Group related bullets** when they form a complete concept (prefer 1-2 bullets per card, max 3)

7. **Extract the AWS service name** that the question is ACTUALLY about (the main subject, not just mentioned in passing)
